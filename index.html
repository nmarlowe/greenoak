Projects


112 Ocean Avenue

The happy couple poses before we did a complete renovation of the basement. Old construction had given way to pests that seemed to take over the whole house in the early hours of the morning. Sadly, they abandoned the house before we completed the job, but the new owners love it!

1428 Elm Street

The new owners of this classic brought us in for general renovations that included removing bars from the windows (such a nice neighborhood too) and new sheetrock in one of the bedrooms. Strangely enough, the old sheetrock almost looked like it had stretched outwards, but it's good as new now. And, as luck would have it, we were commissioned for another job right down the road to upgrade a basement furnace to a more modern central air system.

Fairvale, California

This wonderful old house looks rough on the outside, but don't let that fool you. The owner, a quiet and super nice gentleman, was looking to remodel the master suite on the top floor. He asked for a room becoming of a grand matriarch and we delivered. His mother was out of town during the renovation, but we hear she loved it!
